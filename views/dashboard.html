Quick Content Tips

Hook immediately. Put the strongest visual and sentence in the first two seconds.

Keep one goal. Each post should teach one thing and end with one clear call to action.

Use the right shape. Posts generate square; Reels generate vertical video and a vertical cover.

Preview before paying. Four-scene Reel storyboards are free. Paid video rendering appears only after you review the scenes and wording.

Keep spelling exact. Paid videos intentionally avoid AI-rendered lettering; use the supplied overlay copy and caption.

Media costs: Pictures use your OpenAI balance. Four-scene storyboards are free; an 8-second paid render is approximately $0.80 and requires a second confirmation.

What should we make?
